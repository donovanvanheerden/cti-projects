This is a typical day at AMA-Zing Airways Airport

Video Description

00:00 - 00:07 We see an aeroplane being moved into position to prepare for take off!

00:08 - 00:15 We see an aeroplane come in to an "off-loading-bay", the passengers arriving may get off.

00:16 - 00:31 We see the general background work of the airport staff checking the aeroplane and offloading/transporting luggage.

00:32 - 00:47 We see an aeroplane accepting passengers board on, followed by a second plane going to offload passengers. Finally the first plane gets into position for take off.

00:48 - 01:03 We see the general work of the airport staff at night, followed by aeroplanes arriving as well as preparing to leave.

01:04 - 01:34 We see the airport staff working in the early morning moving the plane in preparation for it to take off, until it finally does take off.

01:35 - 01:42 We see the iconic Pokémon branded plane prepare for take off.

01:43 - 01:58 Again we see general work of the airport staff at night, followed by an aeroplane preparing to accept passengers.

01:59 - 02:06 We see the airport staff moving the various vehicles around.

02:07 - 02:14 We see a different view of the airport staff working at night.

02:15 - 02:22 We see the airport staff doing a wheel check on the aeroplane, preparing it for take off.

02:23 - 03:10 We see a view of the airport airstrip and the vast aeroplane traffic, following by a maintenance team doing their checks on an aeroplane.

03:11 - 03:17 Again we see general work of the airport staff at night.

03:18 - 03:28 We see the airport from the outside, near the highway entrance. Where you can see the various traffic entering and exiting the airport.
