Customer Support

How can we help you?

We are sorry when your journey with us doesn't go as smoothly as you would like. When it happens we want you to be able find the information you need quickly and easily, so this page gives you details to help you find out what you can do yourself online and how to contact us.

What if your flight is delayed or cancelled?

If your flight has been cancelled, or you have experienced a significant delay, we will help you re-arrange your travel plans accordingly.

Rebooking Options:

If you are booked to travel on a flight that is cancelled, you can either:

  1. Rebook onto another AMA-Zing Airways flight at a later date at no extra charge and subject to availability.
  2. Cancel your booking and claim a refund to the original form of payment.


If your booking also includes any other AMA-Zing Airways flights that are not cancelled you will be able to change these flights at the same time.


If your booking includes a hotel, a vehicle, a transfer or an experience booked in conjunction with a cancelled flight, you can either:

  • Rebook to travel, on a like for like basis, within a 14 day period after the scheduled departure time of the original flight at no extra charge and subject to availability.
  • Rebook to travel, 15 days or more after the scheduled departure time of the original flight. Rebookings are subject to availability and no amendment fee will be charged. Any difference in the price of your hotel, vehicle, transfer or experience will need to be paid by you or will be refunded to you.
  • Cancel your booking and claim a refund to the original form of payment.

What to do if your baggage is delayed or missing?

If we misplace or lose your baggage, we will make every effort to return it to you as soon as possible.

Finding delayed and lost baggage

The sooner you report a problem to us, the quicker we can resolve it. That's why if your bags have not arrived on time, it's really important that you tell us about it.

If you reported a problem with your baggage at the airport and have a baggage file reference number you do not need to take any further action.
(A baggage file reference number is a series of five letters and five numbers such as FGKZA92648 and is different to the baggage receipt you were given when checking your bags in.)


If you left the airport without reporting your problem and do not have a baggage file reference number please take the following steps:

  • Tell us about your delayed baggage.(describe it. ie. colour, size, distinct markings?, etc...).
  • If you were travelling to the Intergalactic Islands from outside of the Nova-Roost Continent, call the AMA-Zing Airways Baggage Support Line on 055-02941-9183-285.

As soon as we find your bag we will return it to you for free to any address you choose. (This service is not available in some countries due to local government restrictions but we will advise you if this applies.)

Claiming for lost baggage, damaged baggage and damaged and missing contents

Before making a claim, please ensure you have told us about your damage or loss and have a reference number (e.g. the baggage file reference number from a delayed bag report):

If you haven't already done so, report your problem now

If you believe you have lost contents or had contents damaged during a baggage inspection you can claim compensation directly from the AMA-Zing Transport Security Administration (ATSA):

ATSA claim form

A personal travel insurance policy will likely provide a greater level of cover than we can offer. We therefore recommend that you contact your insurer before making a claim directly with us. We will provide you with a written report, on request, to send to your insurer as proof of loss, damage or delay to your baggage, in support of your claim.

If you do not have travel insurance that covers you for any relevant loss, you may claim from us for the following reasons within the set deadlines:

Reason for claim Deadline for claim
Baggage reported as missing for more than three days 21 days from the date of the flight
Expenses incurred because of a delay to a bag 21 days from the date of the flight
Damage to a bag 7 days from the date of the flight
Missing or damaged baggage contents 7 days from the date of the flight
